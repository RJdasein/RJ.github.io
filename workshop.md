# Workshops

Movement is one of the ways I come into relationship with myself, others, and the world.

My workshops integrate Dance Movement Therapy, psychomotor practice, expressive arts, and embodied exploration. Each gathering is an invitation to slow down, listen, create, and connect.

# Workshop Archive

### 🌸 Spring Dance（春之舞）

A seasonal movement workshop celebrating spring, renewal, and the body's natural rhythm.

Through movement, music, and shared presence, participants explored how the changing seasons can be experienced within the body.

**Themes**

- Renewal
- Creativity
- Body awareness
- Group connection

---

### 🌱 Sheng Sheng（生生）

An embodied movement workshop inspired by the Chinese concept of **"生生"** — life continuously giving rise to life.

Participants explored the relationship between body, emotion, imagination, and the vitality of everyday existence.

**Themes**

- Embodiment
- Authentic movement
- Vitality
- Collective reflection

---

### 🔥 Burning Without Knowing（不知燃烧）

An improvisational movement workshop exploring passion, uncertainty, and transformation.

Through movement, silence, and creative expression, participants were invited to reconnect with the inner forces that continue to move us forward.

**Themes**

- Improvisation
- Emotional expression
- Creativity
- Transformation

# Embodied Cities

Movement has also become part of the way I travel.

Over the years, I have initiated spontaneous movement practices and dance flash mobs in public spaces, exploring the relationship between body, city, and everyday life.

### Selected Cities

- 🇹🇷 Istanbul
- 🇫🇷 Paris
- 🇨🇳 Shanghai
- 🇮🇩 Bali
- 🇻🇳 Hanoi

Each city carries its own rhythm.
Each encounter leaves a different movement in the body.

This ongoing project continues wherever life takes me.
